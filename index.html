About the author

Pastor Samuel Victor Obembe

Pastor Samuel Victor Obembe is a pastor in the Redeemed Christian Church of God, a teacher of the Word, and a passionate ambassador of the gospel of Jesus Christ. He holds a Master of Arts degree, among other academic qualifications, and is committed to continuous learning, spiritual growth, and the advancement of God’s Kingdom.

By the grace of God, his ministry has been a source of blessing to many, particularly through counselling, teaching, and spiritual guidance, bringing encouragement, transformation, and renewed hope to lives. He is deeply passionate about raising young people to discover their God-given purpose and fulfil their divine destinies.

He is happily married to Pastor (Mrs.) Yinka Mercy Obembe, and they are blessed with both biological and spiritual children.

Pastor Obembe is the author of No More Barrenness, A Minister of God, Purposeful Living, and several other inspiring works dedicated to spiritual growth, faith, and purposeful living.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know.

Is every physical copy signed?

Yes. Every launch copy is personally signed and may include the recipient’s name or a short inscription request.

Can I request a particular inscription?

Yes. A short request can be supplied during checkout.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery timing will depend on the buyer’s location and the confirmed dispatch schedule.

How will I receive the e-book?

An automated email containing your secure, personal download link will be sent to the email address you provided during checkout immediately after payment is confirmed.

What format is the e-book?

The e-book is delivered as a high-quality PDF, designed to be beautifully readable on digital screens.

Can I read it on my phone or tablet?

Yes. You can open and read the PDF on any modern smartphone, tablet, e-reader, or computer.

How long does delivery take after payment?

Delivery is instant. As soon as your payment is processed, our system automatically triggers the delivery email.

What should I do if I do not receive the email?

First, check your Spam, Junk, or Promotions tab. If it is not there, please contact us at hello@purposefullivingbook.com with your order reference.

Can I share the download link?

No. Your download link is unique, cryptographically signed, and is linked to your email address. It expires after 48 hours and is limited to a maximum of 5 downloads to prevent unauthorized sharing.

What happens if my payment is debited but access is not delivered?

Do not worry. Our systems verify transactions automatically. If a network delay occurs, simply email hello@purposefullivingbook.com with your payment reference, and our support team will resolve it for you.
